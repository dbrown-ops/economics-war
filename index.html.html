◊ Diplomatic Window

Open a 3-minute negotiation window. All wars and trades freeze. Students can ONLY send DMs and propose alliances. Use this in class to force focused face-to-face diplomacy.

Trigger Global Events

Targeted Event — Apply to One Student

Hit a single student with any event. Use for individualized teaching moments — "your country just had an oil discovery" or "your shipping lane is blocked." The same event still applies its bonus or penalty depending on the student's profile.

◊ Custom Broadcast

Teacher Controls

Add Student Country

Paste a row from your spreadsheet. The country gets added immediately, locked in, and placed on the map. Country name, real name, export, and import are permanent — but you can move them between continents anytime using the Move Country panel.

→ Paste a block per student. Each block starts with the student's real name on its own line, followed by labeled fields (Country Name, GDP, Major Export, Major Import, Continent, etc.). Paste multiple blocks at once to add a whole class.

→ GDP accepts shorthand: "50B", "50 billion", "$50.00 billion", "750M", "1.5T" all work.

→ Required fields: Real Name (first line), Country Name, Continent, Major Export, Major Import. Optional: GDP (defaults to $500), National Vulnerability, Global Strategy Goal, Student Handle.

◊ Manual Action Entry

Pick which country is acting, then choose the action. The simulation will update as if the student did it themselves.

◊ Quick Actions for Any Country

One-click shortcuts for common in-class moments.

◊ Roster & GDP Adjustment

Add or subtract from any player's totals. Use this when a student deserves a manual bump (extra credit, in-class assignment) or a correction. Auto-tick runs every 10 minutes adding +0.5% of import/export targets and +0.10% of GDP target to every player automatically — this is on top of that.

Alliance Manager

Form an alliance between any two parties. Pick the joiner, then pick who they're allying with — a solo country OR an existing alliance group. To switch from one alliance to another, pick "Leave current and join…" — this triggers a broken trust penalty.

Leave an Alliance

Removes one country from a group. Triggers broken-trust penalty (the leaver and remaining members can never re-ally).

Move Country to a Different Continent

Move a student's country if they switched alliances or you want to rebalance the map. Country name, real name, export, and import stay locked.

Battle Simulator — Best of 3

Tick the countries on each side. A country can only be on ONE side — checking attacker auto-unchecks defender, and vice versa. Each student rolls one die per round. Best of 3 rounds wins.

🚫 IMPOSE SANCTIONS

Sanctions block all trade between you and the target for 3 rounds. The target loses $100 immediately; you lose $40 (sanctions cost the sender too). This is public — everyone will see it.

Use this when you want to apply economic pressure without going to war.

💼 INVEST IN YOUR ECONOMY

Spend $150 of GDP now to gain a long-term economic advantage. Choose carefully.

🏭 Production Capacity

Future export sales earn +15% more (stacks). Pays off if you trade often.

🌐 Supply Chain Diversification

Resets your import dependency. Use this if one country supplies too much of your imports — protects you from embargoes.

⚔ DECLARE WAR

WARNING: Declaring war is public. The defender will be notified, your alliances will be called to fight WITH you, and the dice will roll. The loser pays $200 GDP. Allies who refuse to fight lose their alliance with you forever.

✉ Send Secret Message

Only you and the recipient see this. The teacher also sees ALL secret messages.
